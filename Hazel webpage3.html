Getting Started with HazelNut

Step 1: Invite HazelNut to Your Server

Click on the "Invite HazelNut Now" button from the home page to add HazelNut to your Discord server.

Step 2: Use the Help Command

Type ;help in your server to see a list of available commands and how to use them.

Step 3: Complete Tasks

Use the ;task command and complete the given task to earn more!

Step 4: Play Trivia Games

Start a trivia game by typing ;trivia. Compete with your friends, climb the leaderboard, and earn credits!

Step 5: Invest in stocks

Checkout the available stocks using ;stocks, Buy and sell them unpo your will!

Step 6: Shopify

Explore tha items available in the ;shop!
