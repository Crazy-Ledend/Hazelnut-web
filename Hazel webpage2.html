Command List

Credits

;task: Earn credits by completing tasks.

;status: View your current Cache and Vault balance.

;store <amt>: Deposit into your Vault.

;extract <amt>: Withdraw from your Vault to your Cache.

;profile: View your credits, inventory, and progress.

;shop: Display items available in the shop.

;purchase <item> / ;p <item>: Buy items with credits from the shop.

;use <item>: Use items in your inventory.

Minigames

;simulate <bet_amt>: Play a random minigame like RPS or HighLow.

;trivia: Answer trivia questions for rewards.

;leaderboard / ;lb: View trivia rankings.

;cf: Flip a coin and predict the outcome.

CryoStock Trading

;stocks: View the list of available CryoStocks.

;buy <Ctk> <qty>: Purchase CryoStocks.

;sell <Ctk> <qty>: Sell CryoStocks in your possession.

;portfolio: View your CryoStocks portfolio.

;virtualize: Visualize value fluctuations of virtual entities.

Fun

;wave <user>: Send a friendly wave.

;pat <user>: Pat someone warmly.

;hug <user>: Give a deep, warm hug.

;kiss <user>: Plant a kiss.

;lick <user>: Playfully lick someone.

;bite <user>: Give a playful bite.

;cuddle <user>: Offer a cozy cuddle.

;slap <user>: Playfully slap someone.

;punch <user>: Deliver a punch.

;kick <user>: Playfully kick someone.

;stab <user>: Pretend to stab someone.

;kill <user>: Pretend to kill someone.
