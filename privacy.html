Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority.

Information Collection

HazelNut collects user IDs, server IDs, and channel IDs to provide personalized functionality. We do not store message content or sensitive personal information.

Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for providing features like trivia, games, and Pokedex functionality. Data is never shared with third parties.

Data Storage

We use secure databases to store data and ensure it is protected against unauthorized access. Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services.

User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. Contact us through our support server to initiate the process.

Third-Party Services

HazelNut integrates with APIs such as the Pokémon API to fetch data for Pokedex functionality. These services have their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our services. Users will be notified of significant updates through our support server.
